September 4th, 2023

David, Samuel, Micah, and Malachi, young kids in the neighborhood, showed up at my door this morning asking to wash my cars for money. They presented themselves more professionally than most adults, so I have them a shot. They are young but worked really hard. I had them vacuum my cars too. Money well spent. I like to encourage that sort of entrepreneurship and hard work in kids. If you need your car washed, call them...
